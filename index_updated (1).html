About Safeera

Safeera connects riders and verified drivers in a safe and simple way. We focus on punctuality, safety checks, and clear communication. Start local, grow responsibly.

Safety & Disclaimer

Disclaimer: Safeera is a technology platform that connects passengers with drivers. Safeera does not own vehicles and is not the service provider. Drivers are independent service providers and are responsible for their vehicles, license and insurance. In case of any accident or damage, liability will be handled through the driver's insurance and relevant authorities. Safeera recommends all drivers to maintain valid insurance and documents.

Our Safety Steps (Free / Immediate)

  • Driver verification: Aadhaar, Driving License, RC, Insurance copy (collect before onboarding).
  • SOS info: Provide police and emergency contacts in the app/website.
  • Support: Use the website contact / WhatsApp for immediate help.

Legal note: This disclaimer is a basic protection. For full legal safety consult a lawyer and register appropriate company entity when you scale.
