Safety & Disclaimer

Disclaimer: Safeera is a technology platform that connects passengers with drivers. Safeera does not own vehicles and is not the service provider. To protect privacy, Safeera will use system-generated virtual numbers (call masking) so drivers and riders do not see each other's real phone numbers.
